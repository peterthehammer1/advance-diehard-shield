Three Intelligent Call Paths

Every inbound call is analyzed by Nucleus AI and routed through one of three paths based on the caller's trust level.

✅

Whitelisted Callers

Always Connected
  1. 1Inbound call arrives — A customer calls the store's protected phone number. Nucleus AI receives the call and notifies the shield system.
  2. 2Caller identified — Nucleus AI looks up the caller's phone number and finds it on the whitelist. The call is classified as trusted.
  3. 3Silent transfer — The AI immediately transfers the call to the store's real landline with no speech. The caller never hears the AI — the experience is seamless.

Blacklisted Callers

Always Blocked
  1. 1Inbound call arrives — A caller dials the store's protected number. Nucleus AI receives the call and checks the caller's status.
  2. 2Caller blocked — The system identifies the number on the blacklist. The caller is routed to the Nucleus AI containment agent.
  3. 3AI engages naturally — Nucleus AI greets the caller and answers store questions (hours, address, parts) — but has no transfer capability. The caller never reaches the store.

Unknown Callers

Screened by AI
  1. 1First-time caller — The caller's number is not on any list. Nucleus AI activates the screening protocol.
  2. 2AI screening begins — Nucleus AI greets the caller naturally and begins real-time voice analysis, evaluating whether the caller is a human or automated system.
  3. 3aHuman detected — If the caller engages naturally, Nucleus AI transfers them to the store and automatically whitelists their number for instant access on future calls.
  4. 3bBot detected — If the caller is silent, plays a recording, or behaves like a robocall, Nucleus AI terminates the call immediately.
